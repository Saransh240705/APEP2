The project aims to overcome common challenges in foot rehabilitation, such as limited effectiveness of conventional methods for foot, lack of personalization, limited options for dynamic resistance. There is no multitasking unit for foot rehab using hydrotherapy. Through the development of the Aqua podiatric exercises platform the project seeks to revolutionize foot rehabilitation by offering assistance and resistance to promote movement and strengthening of foot and enhances the overall rehabilitation experience for patients.

The Aqua Podiatric Rehabilitation Unit is a specialized facility designed to provide holistic foot and ankle care through a combination of advanced water-based therapies and modern rehabilitation tools. The unit is equipped with hydraulic systems, foot arch correctors, hydrotherapy pools, and TheraBand's to deliver effective and gentle treatments that support recovery, strength, and mobility.
